# Coinbase in Canada: 2026 Reference Guide

## Quick Setup Checklist
- [ ] Verify identity (government-issued ID + proof of address)
- [ ] Confirm province of residence (some provinces have restrictions)
- [ ] Link payment method (bank account, debit card, or wire transfer)
- [ ] Enable 2FA for security

## Supported Payment Methods in Canada
| Method | Processing Time | Fees | Notes |
|--------|-----------------|------|-------|
| Bank Account Transfer | 3-5 business days | Lowest | Preferred for larger trades |
| Debit Card | Instant | 1.5-2% | Highest convenience, higher fees |
| Wire Transfer | 1-2 business days | $15 CAD | Good for large deposits |

## Provincial Restrictions (2026)
Each province has different regulatory requirements. Confirm your province before account creation:
- **Alberta, BC, Ontario, Quebec**: Full Coinbase access
- **Other provinces**: Verify current status with Coinbase support

## Fee Structure
- **Trading (Maker)**: 0.1-0.6%
- **Trading (Taker)**: 0.15-0.6%
- **Deposits**: Varies by method (0-2%)
- **Withdrawals**: Network-dependent (crypto) or flat fee (CAD)

## Tax Considerations for Canadian Traders
- **Capital Gains Tax**: 50% of gains subject to income tax
- **Reporting**: Must report all transactions to CRA
- **Record-Keeping**: Maintain detailed buy/sell records for 6 years
- **ACB Method**: Most common adjusted cost base method for Canada

## Security Best Practices
1. Enable 2FA (authenticator app preferred over SMS)
2. Use a strong, unique password
3. Whitelist withdrawal addresses
4. Monitor account activity regularly
5. Consider hardware wallet for long-term holdings

## Useful Resources
- CRA Crypto Tax Guidelines (T776 for business, Schedule 3 for personal)
- Coinbase Help Center for Canada-specific issues
- Provincial securities commission websites for local regulations
