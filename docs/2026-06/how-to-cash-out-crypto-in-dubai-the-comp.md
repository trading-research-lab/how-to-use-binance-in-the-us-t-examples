## Tax & Compliance Status (Dubai/UAE)

### Residents
- **Income Tax:** 0% on crypto gains (as of 2026)
- **Reporting:** Recommended for bank compliance
- **Limits:** No formal daily/monthly limits; banks monitor for AML

### Non-Residents
- **Tax Liability:** 0% on UAE earnings (tax treaty dependent)
- **Documentation:** Visa copy, address proof required
- **Restrictions:** Some platforms limit non-resident volumes

## Cashout Process Checklist

- [ ] Verify account residency status & tier
- [ ] Confirm IBAN/bank details linked to same identity
- [ ] Check current withdrawal limits (varies by platform)
- [ ] Review current AED/USD spot rates
- [ ] Submit withdrawal request
- [ ] Confirm via 2FA
- [ ] Track reference number (typically arrives 1-3 days)

## Key Considerations

1. **Bank Friction:** Many UAE traditional banks require crypto account holder to provide receipts; prepare transaction history
2. **Stablecoin Route:** USDC/USDT withdrawal often faster than volatile assets
3. **Peer Exchanges:** P2P platforms (unregulated) exist but carry counterparty risk
4. **Documentation:** Keep proof of fund origin (exchange trading history, wallet receipts)
