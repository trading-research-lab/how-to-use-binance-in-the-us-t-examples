# Coinbase India Setup & Compliance Reference

## Quick Eligibility Checklist
- [ ] Age: 18+ (required)
- [ ] Indian resident with valid ID (Aadhaar / PAN preferred)
- [ ] Bank account or UPI linked to your name
- [ ] Compliance with India's crypto tax filing obligations (Schedule FA)

## KYC Document Requirements
| Document Type | Status | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Government ID (Aadhaar/PAN) | Required | Photo ID must match account name |
| Address Proof (Bank Statement / Utility Bill) | Required | Must be dated within last 3 months |
| PAN Card | Recommended | Faster tax ID verification |
| Source of Funds Declaration | May be required | For accounts >₹10L transactions/year |

## INR Deposit Methods (2026)
| Method | Processing Time | Fees | Minimum |
|---|---|---|---|
| Bank Transfer (NEFT/RTGS) | 1-2 hours | ~₹0 | ₹1,000 |
| UPI (P2P) | Instant | ~1-2% | ₹500 |
| Debit Card | Instant | 1.49% | ₹1,000 |

## Coinbase Fee Structure (India)
| Service | Standard Maker Fee | Standard Taker Fee |
|---|---|---|
| Spot Trading | 0.40% | 0.60% |
| Staking Rewards | Platform dependent | ~10-15% annual |
| Withdrawal (INR) | ₹0-₹10 | Varies by bank |

## Tax Filing Obligations
- **Schedule FA (Form ITTR)**: Report all crypto holdings at FY-end (amt in USD or INR equivalent)
- **Capital Gains Tax**: Short-term (STTC) 30%, Long-term (LTCG) 20% (after indexation) if held >2 years
- **TDS**: 1% TDS on crypto trades >₹100K in a FY (if applicable)
- **Reporting Deadline**: 31 July (ITR filing)

## Account Restrictions & Compliance
- Coinbase may request additional verification for large transactions
- India does not ban crypto trading (as of 2026), but RBI guidance applies
- Self-certification of tax compliance may be required
- Account may be flagged for sanctions screening

## Regional Availability
- ✅ Coinbase Direct (spot trading) available
- ⚠️ Coinbase Advanced (derivatives) may have restrictions
- ✅ Staking services available to eligible Indian residents
- ⚠️ Futures trading restricted in some regions
