## KYC & Account Verification Requirements

- **Tier 1 (Unverified):** Limited to $0 withdrawal (typically no trading allowed)
- **Tier 2 (Verified):** Full KYC submission required
  - Government-issued ID
  - Proof of residence
  - Personal details (name, DOB, address)
  - ~24-48 hour review
- **Tier 3 (Enhanced):** Additional documentation for high-volume trading

## Compliance Checklist

- [ ] Confirm your state is not on exclusion list
- [ ] Prepare government-issued photo ID
- [ ] Gather proof of US residence (utility bill, bank statement <90 days old)
- [ ] Verify income/employment status if requested
- [ ] Accept Binance.US terms and regulatory agreements
- [ ] Enable 2FA before first deposit
- [ ] Understand tax reporting obligations (1099-K at $20k+ or 200+ transactions)

## Key Regulatory Notes

- Binance.US is a separate, US-regulated entity (regulated by FinCEN as MSB)
- Cannot use international Binance platform from US without legal risk
- Tax reporting: Keep records of all buy/sell dates, amounts, and prices
- Withdrawal limits may apply based on verification tier
